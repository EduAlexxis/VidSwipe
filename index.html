Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 5, 2025

Your privacy is our top priority. ReelVault was built with a "Local First" architecture.

1. Data Collection

ReelVault requires access to your Apple Photo Library (`PHPhotoLibrary`) to display your videos. This access is strictly read-and-write to allow you to favorite or delete assets at your request.

2. Location Services

We use Location Services to display the city and country where your videos were taken. This data is processed locally on your device using Apple's `CLGeocoder`. We do not store your location history on any external servers.

3. Data Storage

Metadata such as custom tags are stored locally on your device using `UserDefaults`. Your video files remain in your Apple Photos library.

4. Third-Party Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or transfer your data to outside parties. The app does not utilize any third-party analytics SDKs that track user behavior.
